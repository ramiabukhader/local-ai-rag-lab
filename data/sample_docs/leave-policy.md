# Leave Policy

## Annual leave
Full-time employees are entitled to 25 days of annual leave per calendar year, in
addition to public holidays. Part-time employees receive a pro-rata entitlement.
This is a fictional sample document used only to exercise the lab.

## Requesting leave
Annual leave must be requested through the HR system and approved by your line
manager at least five working days in advance.

## Carrying leave over
Up to 5 days of unused annual leave may be carried over into the following year.
Any remaining balance beyond that is forfeited.

## Sick leave
Employees who are unwell should notify their manager as early as possible. A
self-certification form is required for absences of up to five days.
