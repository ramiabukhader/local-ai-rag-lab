# Expense Policy

## Purpose
This policy explains how employees claim work-related expenses and the limits
that apply. It is a fictional sample document used only to exercise the lab.

## Meal expenses
Employees may claim meal expenses while travelling on company business. The daily
meal expense limit is 40 units per day. Receipts are required for any single meal
over 15 units. Alcohol is not reimbursable.

## Travel
Standard-class travel should be booked in advance where possible. Taxi fares are
reimbursable for journeys where public transport is impractical.

## Approval and reimbursement
All expense claims must be submitted within 30 days and approved by the employee's
line manager before reimbursement. Claims submitted without a valid receipt may be
rejected.
